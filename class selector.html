ABESEC

ABES Engineering College (ABES EC) is a well-known private engineering institution located in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, in the National Capital Region (NCR) of India. Established in 2000, the college is affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), Lucknow, and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and management. Popular B.Tech branches include Computer Science & Engineering, Information Technology, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Electrical & Electronics Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Civil Engineering. ABES also offers MBA and M.Tech programs. ABES Engineering College has a modern campus with well-equipped laboratories, smart classrooms, a central library, hostels, sports facilities, and cafeterias. The Training and Placement Cell helps students secure jobs in reputed companies.
