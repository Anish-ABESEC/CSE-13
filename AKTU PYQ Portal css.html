Engineering Mathematics

Chapter: Matrices

  • Find the inverse of a matrix.
  • Solve system of equations using matrix method.
  • Find eigen values of a matrix.
  • Verify Cayley–Hamilton theorem.
  • Find the rank of a matrix.

Chapter: Differential Calculus

  • Find limits of functions.
  • Differentiate implicit functions.
  • Find maxima and minima.

Chapter: Integral Calculus

  • Evaluate definite integrals.
  • Find area between curves.

Engineering Physics

Chapter: Quantum Mechanics

  • Explain Heisenberg uncertainty principle.
  • Derive Schrödinger wave equation.
  • Explain particle in a box.

Chapter: Wave Optics

  • Explain single slit diffraction.
  • Derive expression for fringe width.
  • Explain diffraction grating.

Chapter: Fiber Optics & Laser

  • Explain principle of optical fiber.
  • Describe construction of laser.

Electrical Engineering

Chapter: DC Circuits

  • State and explain Kirchhoff’s laws.
  • Solve circuits using nodal analysis.

Chapter: AC Circuits

  • Explain RLC series circuit.
  • Define power factor and its importance.

Chapter: Transformers

  • Explain working principle of transformer.
  • Derive EMF equation of transformer.
